How to use?

  1. Sign up or log in to your iBill account to securely access and manage your invoices.
  2. Click the “Generate your invoice here!” button to open the invoice creation form.
  3. Enter your business details including name, email, address, country, and phone number.
  4. Fill in customer details such as name, email, address, and phone number (if applicable).
  5. Add one or more items with quantity, rate, and tax percentage as needed.
  6. Review the calculated subtotal and total amounts displayed below the item section.
  7. Click the “Generate Document” button to create your invoice.
  8. Your invoice will be available for download and stored in your Profile for future access.
  9. Visit the Profile page anytime to view or download your previously created invoices.
